
COMANDS:

nmap (to check if installed)
1. Basic Open Port Scan
nmap www.facebook.com
2. Specific Port Scan
nmap -p 80,443 www.facebook.com
3. TCP Port Scan
nmap -sT www.facebook.com
4. UDP Port Scan
nmap -sU www.facebook.com
5. OS Fingerprinting
nmap -O www.facebook.com
6. Ping Scan
nmap -sn www.facebook.com
7. Aggressive Scan
nmap -A www.facebook.com
8. Scan a Range of IPs
nmap www.facebook.com-www.amazon.com
9. Scan a Subnet
nmap <subnet>/24
nmap 192.168.1.45
10. Save Scan Results to a File
nmap -oN output.txt www.facebook.com
